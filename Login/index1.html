About Us

Advanced Chatbot is a modern web application developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, integrated with Google's Gemini to provide intelligent and context-aware responses. The project bridges the gap between users and cutting-edge technology through a simple, accessible chat interface.

Our platform is built with a focus on usability and security. Users can register and log in to access a personalized chatbot experience. The application supports text-based conversations as well as image-based queries, making it versatile for a wide range of use cases — from learning and research to creative brainstorming and daily problem-solving.

Our mission is to make communication available to everyone through an efficient, interactive, and easy-to-use platform that feels natural and responsive.
